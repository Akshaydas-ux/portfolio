ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Akshay Das – a dedicated graphic designer who transforms ideas into visually impactful solutions. My focus is on creating designs that are both striking and thoughtfully strategic.

My creative journey started from a simple project and evolved into a passion for using industry-leading tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Animate CC, Canva, and Figma. I apply my expertise to everything from logos and posters to dynamic digital art.

When I’m not designing, I’m likely exploring new trends or diving into movies and anime. I’m always eager to learn and grow in my craft.
